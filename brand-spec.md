## Observed Rules

1. Use black or photographic backgrounds with a black overlay; never use a light page canvas.
2. Headings are uppercase, serif-like, cinematic, and set in the parchment accent color.
3. Body copy is narrow, condensed-feeling, and light on dark; spacing is editorial rather than SaaS-card based.
4. Layout posture is documentary dossier: large hero, anchored single-page navigation, synopsis, technical ficha, visual treatment, participants, production plan, and contact.
5. Image treatment should feel archival: grain, desaturation/sepia, low contrast, and dark vignettes; decorative color is avoided.
